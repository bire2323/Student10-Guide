Natural and Social Sciences in Ethiopia

Natural Sciences

University/College Departments Primary Work Areas
Addis Ababa University - College of Natural and Computational Sciences Biology, Chemistry, Computer Science, Geology, Mathematics, Physics, Statistics Research in computational modeling, geophysics, bioinformatics; programs in environmental science and data analysis; focus on sustainable development and innovation.
Jimma University - College of Natural Sciences Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, Physics, Statistics, Sport Science Emphasis on health-related sciences, statistical modeling for agriculture, and physics applications in energy; research in tropical diseases and ecosystem management.
Arba Minch University - College of Natural Sciences Biology, Chemistry, Geology, Physics Demand-driven research in biological sciences, water resources, and earth sciences; focus on responsible citizenship and sustainability.
Wollo University - College of Natural Science Biology, Chemistry, Geology, Mathematics, Physics, Sport Science Postgraduate programs in regular, summer, and extension modes; research in applied geology and sports health.
Adigrat University - College of Natural Sciences Biology, Biotechnology, Chemistry, Geology, Mathematics, Physics, Sport Science Undergraduate focus on biotechnology applications, mineral exploration, and mathematical modeling for local industries.
Dilla University - College of Natural and Computational Science Applied Biology, Applied Chemistry, Geology, Applied Mathematics, Statistics, Applied Sport Sciences, Applied Physics Applied research in environmental geology, statistical analysis for development, and sports physiology.
Wolkite University - Natural & Computational Sciences Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, Physics, Statistics, Geology, Computer Science Industry collaboration in areas like computational chemistry and biological conservation.
Adama Science and Technology University - School of Applied Natural Science Applied Biology, Applied Chemistry, Industrial Chemistry, Biotechnology, Environmental Science Programs in industrial applications, chemical engineering, and environmental protection.

Social Sciences

University/College Departments Primary Work Areas
Addis Ababa University - College of Social Sciences Archaeology and Heritage Management, Geography and Environmental Studies, History, Political Science and International Relations, Social Anthropology, Sociology Focus on heritage preservation, geopolitical analysis, and social dynamics; research in urban studies and conflict resolution.
Haramaya University - College of Social Sciences and Humanities Sociology, Geography, History, Political Science, English Language and Literature Interdisciplinary research in education, empowerment, language, culture, and governance; aims for leading role in East Africa.
Jimma University - College of Social Sciences and Humanities Sociology, Law (integrated), History Graduate studies in social justice, legal research, and community development.
Wollo University - College of Social Science and Humanities Geography, History and Heritage Management, Sociology, Political Science, English, Journalism Quality education in humanities; research on regional histories and media studies.
Arba Minch University - College of Social Sciences and Humanities Sociology, Geography, History, Political Science Research in social justice, peace and conflict studies, and community engagement.
Debre Berhan University - College of Social Sciences & Humanities Amharic Language & Literature, English Language & Literature, Geography & Environmental Studies, History & Heritage Management, Political Science & International Relations, Social Anthropology, Sociology Studies in language preservation, environmental policy, and anthropological fieldwork.
Jigjiga University - College of Social Sciences and Humanities Sociology, Journalism and Communications, Ethiopian Languages and Literature, English Language and Literature, Political Science Undergraduate programs in media, linguistics, and governance; focus on Somali region contexts.
Dilla University - College of Social Science And Humanities Intercultural Relations and Governance, Peace and Conflict Studies, Teaching Amharic Research in peacebuilding, governance, and Amharic language education.
